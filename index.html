Our SkinIn app is designed to provide quick and easy access to information about active ingredients in skincare products. These are the ingredients that are intended to treat a specific problem and have been scientifically proven.

With just a few taps, our app can help you find out what a particular ingredient does, whether it's suitable for your skin type, and how it works with other ingredients.
